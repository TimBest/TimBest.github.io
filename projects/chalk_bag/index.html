First Attempt: The Moose Bag

To make this bag I followed a pattern found at craftsy.com with the following modifications:

  • Lengthened the bag's lining to eliminate a sizable separation between the lining and the shell.
  • Added clear nail polish to the ends of the draw string and ribbons to prevent them from fraying.
  • Cut out and ironed on a moose stencil to the outside of the bag.
Other then that the pattern worked as expected and allowed me the make a great bag.
