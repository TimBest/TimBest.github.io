Composers Couch

BitBucket Repo

About

A website dedicated to providing the tools necessary to book and promote concerts.

Authentication

'Sign up' and 'Login' terminology was used they are the two most distinct phrases that describe the process. They differ in number of words and the starting letter.

  • Homepage contains the 'Sign up' and 'Login' forms.

  • Users can authenticate through Facebook, Twitter or an email address and password.

  • Zipcode auto filled based on the users IP address. future plans were made to convert zip-code to city and then eventually remove it from the login.

  • After social authentication users were prompted to give their name and location.

Inbox

The users messages, private requests, public requests and show notifications are managed here.

  • All messages sent and replied to by the user can be seen here.

Calendar

Used to manage confirmed shows and private requests. Shows are exportable.

  • Form used to add or edit shows.

  • Single day view.

  • Month view view.

  • Expanded days allow for more detail on smaller screen, quick access to artist/venue search, and a create show button with the corresponding date already filled in.

  • Can view the entire year and quickly jump to a month, week, day or single event.
