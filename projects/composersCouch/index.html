Composers Couch

BitBucket Repo

About

A website dedicated to providing the tools necessary to book and promote concerts.

Authentication

'Sign up' and 'Login' terminology were chosen for their distinctness. They differ in number of words and the starting letter allowing for quick scanning of the website.
