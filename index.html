About Me

My name in Tim Best. I am a software developer who graduated from Clarkson University with a Bachelor of Science in computer science and a minor in mathematics. For the past year and a half I have worked on the start up Composer's Couch, a website dedicated to improving how concerts are booked and promoted. While working on this project I enjoyed learning Python, executing best practices for Django, and researching how the music industry functions. Through this experience I have developed a stronger sense of self motivation, improved my visual communication skills, and have acquired a deeper understanding of end user needs.
